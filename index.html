About The Kenrick Centre

Our History

The Kenrick Centre was established in 1985 as a cornerstone of Birmingham City Council's commitment to providing exceptional care for the elderly in our community. Located in the heart of Harborne, Birmingham, the centre was founded to address the growing need for compassionate, high-quality TRE. Over the years, it has grown into a trusted hub for elderly support, offering a range of services tailored to enhance the well-being and independence of our residents. With a legacy of care spanning nearly four decades, The Kenrick Centre remains dedicated to uplifting the lives of seniors through personalized and community-focused services.

Our Mission & Vision

At The Kenrick Centre, our mission is to provide compassionate, holistic care that empowers elderly individuals to live with dignity, comfort, and joy. We strive to create a nurturing environment where seniors feel valued and supported, offering personalized health, social, and emotional care to meet their unique needs. Our commitment is to enhance the quality of life for every individual we serve while fostering a sense of community and belonging.
