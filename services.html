Our Comprehensive Services

At TRE Geriatric Care, we offer a wide range of services designed to improve the lives of the elderly, enhance their health, and help them live independently. We welcome you to reach out to learn more about how we can support you or your loved ones with compassionate and professional care.

1. Home Health Care

  • In-home medical assistance, such as medication management, wound care, dialysis, or IV therapy.
  • Involvement of geriatric nurses or physical therapists.

2. Activities of Daily Living (ADL) Support

  • Assistance for the elderly with basic activities like bathing, dressing, eating, and mobility.
  • Includes personal assistance such as changing clothes or body hygiene.

3. Nutrition and Meal Services

  • Preparation of healthy meals or home-delivered meals.
  • Companies like Senior Helpers can provide meal plans tailored to the health needs of the elderly.

4. Money Management

  • Assisting the elderly with managing bills, budgeting, or long-term care insurance issues.
  • Some companies offer financial management services for seniors needing financial assistance.

5. Transportation Services

  • Providing transportation for the elderly to hospitals, shops, or community centers.
  • These helps elderly individuals who are unable to drive or use public transport.

6. Physical and Occupational Therapy

  • Physical therapy or occupational therapy services to help the elderly regain strength, balance, or daily skills.
  • These can be provided at home or at elderly centers.

7. Companionship and Social Support

  • Providing emotional companionship for lonely seniors through conversation, games, or recreational activities.
  • Companies like Visiting Angels emphasize the importance of enhancing the social lives of seniors.

8. Elderly Care Management (TRE Management)

  • Developing personalized care plans, coordinating with doctors, or collaborating with elderly families.
  • Professionals like care managers can assist with health and housing decisions.

9. Mental Health Services

  • Psychological support for the elderly facing depression, anxiety, or conditions like Alzheimer's or dementia.
  • This may include counseling or behavioral therapy.

10. Adult Day Services

  • Day centers provide a safe environment for the elderly during the day, including social activities, meals, and health services.
  • These are suitable for seniors who need daytime supervision but can return home in the evening.

11. Housing Support

  • Companies can assist in finding suitable housing for the elderly, such as assisted living homes or long-term care facilities (nursing homes).
  • This includes advice on housing options that match the needs of the elderly.
