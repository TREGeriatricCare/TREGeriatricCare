About Us

The Kenrick Centre: A Brief History

The Kenrick Centre was established in 1985 as a cornerstone of Birmingham City Council's commitment to providing exceptional care for the elderly in our community. Located in the heart of Harborne, Birmingham, the centre was founded to address the growing need for compassionate, high-quality TRE.

Over the years, it has grown into a trusted hub for elderly support, offering a range of services tailored to enhance the well-being and independence of our residents. With a legacy of care spanning nearly four decades, The Kenrick Centre remains dedicated to uplifting the lives of seniors through personalized and community-focused services. We pride ourselves on creating a nurturing and supportive environment where every individual feels valued and empowered.

Our Mission

At The Kenrick Centre, our mission is to provide compassionate, holistic care that empowers elderly individuals to live with dignity, comfort, and joy. We strive to create a nurturing environment where seniors feel valued and supported, offering personalized health, social, and emotional care to meet their unique needs. Our commitment is to enhance the quality of life for every individual we serve while fostering a sense of community and belonging.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be Birmingham's leading TRE provider, setting the standard for excellence in elderly care. We aim to create a future where every senior in our community has access to comprehensive, innovative, and empathetic care services that promote independence and well-being. The Kenrick Centre aspires to be a beacon of hope and support, ensuring that aging is a celebrated and fulfilling stage of life.
